Date Caramel

Ingredients

  • 20 pitted Medjool dates.
  • 2/3 to 3/4 cup hot oat milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of sea salt, optional

Instructions

  1. Soak the dates: Place the pitted dates in a bowl and cover them with hot water.

  2. Drain the dates: After soaking, drain the dates of water.

  3. Eat the dates: Just stop cooking and eat your food.
