Yord Fandar

Yord Fandar is a fictional Jedi Master from the Star Wars universe.

The character is played by actor Charlie Barnett in the Disney + web series The Acolyte.

Concept and creation

The character was introduced in the first episode of the Disney+ web series The Acolyte , where he is introduced as a Jedi Master who leads the search mission to find Verosha Aniseya, who is suspected of murdering Jedi Master Indara. 2 The character is played by actor Charlie Barnett.

Fictional biography

Yord Fandar was trained as a Padawan during the High Republic . He became best friends with Verosha Aniseya and, in 232 BBY, achieved the rank of Jedi Master. Two years later he took in the Zygerrian Tasi Lowa as a padawan. 4 Years after Verosha left the order, Yord and Tasi were sent for arrest after being framed for the murder of Master Indara. Osha was detained and sent on a cell ship to Coruscant for interrogation. After a mutiny caused the ship to crash on an ice planet, leadership of the mission passed to Master Sol, who took Yord with him. 5 Once the group, made up of Yord, Sol and the padawan Jecki Lon, found Osha, they peacefully took her with them, also demonstrating that she was not responsible for the crimes, but that the real murderer had been her sister Mei, who was killed. I thought she had been dead for years. Yord began to regain his trust in Osha and confronted Mei shortly after she murdered Master Torbin, even though she ended up escaping from her.
